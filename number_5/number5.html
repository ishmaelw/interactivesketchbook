The verb I am encoding is "holding". The sketch only moves forward if the mouse is held down long enough, which is controlled by the `if (mouseIsPressed && steps, maxSteps)` and `if (holdTime > holdThreshold)` lines in the code. Over time, the progress fills up as `holdTime` increases until the progress bar is completed. The extra "else" step makes it so if the mouse isn't being held down the progress bar goes back down.
